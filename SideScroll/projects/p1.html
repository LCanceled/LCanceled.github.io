Ocean and Cloud Rendering

9/16/2013


As part of a final project for a class, I worked on the advanced graphical aspects which included an ocean as well clouds. These two effects were based off of the papers Simulating Ocean Water by Jerry Tessendorf, Animation and Display of Water by Stefano Lanza, and Real-Time Cloud Rendering for Games by Mark J. Harris. Direct3D10 was used for rendering.
