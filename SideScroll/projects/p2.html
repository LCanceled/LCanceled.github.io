Ray Tracing

9/16/2013


For a brief winter break project, I implemented a recursive multithreaded ray tracer capable of handling arbitrary polygonal soups and dielectrics with total internal reflections. The ray tracer uses a bounding volume hierarchy for collision detection and supports several methods of sampling.
